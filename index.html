About Me - Jordy Spencer

Welcome!

I'm Jordy Spencer, a PhD researcher focusing on pedestrians' visual behavior at crossings using eye-tracking, EEG, and EDA.

My research explores how individuals employ visual attention and decision-making in real-world settings.

Feel free to connect with me to discuss research, collaborations, or anything related to cognitive science and human behavior.
